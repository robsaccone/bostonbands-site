# BostonBands.com Archive

**1996 - 2026 | 30 Years of Local Music**

This is the archive site for [BostonBands.com](https://robsaccone.github.io/bostonbands-site/), a community platform that connected Boston-area musicians, venues, and fans for three decades.

## What Was BostonBands?

Founded in 1996 by Alex Willis, BostonBands.com was the largest online community for New England local music. Over 30 years it grew to include:

- **13,564** bands and artists
- **6,783** music venues
- **4,353** original songs
- **36,001** gig listings
- **8,028** registered members

From basement shows in Allston to stages at the Paradise Rock Club and Middle East, if it was local and it rocked, it was on BostonBands.

## About This Site

This is a static archive site preserving the history and legacy of BostonBands.com. It features real data, photos, and song listings from the original platform.

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build step. Hosted on GitHub Pages.

## Running Locally

Open `index.html` in a browser. That's it.
